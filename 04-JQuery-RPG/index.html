Instructions


This is a game. You are about to run in the presidental elections, and will face off against your political rivals.

Throughout the game, your power will constantly build (your attack strength will increase every round).

Luckily, your opponents lack substance, and their attacks will always do the same amount of damage.

Use this to your advantage.

Start by challenging weaker opponents, and build up your strength over the course of the game to be able to quickly defeat stronger opponents.

Good luck, have fun, and don't forget to turn on your speakers!
