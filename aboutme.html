About Me

My name is David Ellis and I am an aspiring Full Stack Web Developer. I have recently completed the University Of Denver Full Stack Web Development boot camp, where I developed a passion for coding that I am now excitedly pursuing with further projects (currently a React/Expo mobile app). I am always eager to learn something new and collaborate, so feel free to reach out to me using the contact page!
