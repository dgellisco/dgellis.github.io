Anthony Bourdain had a passion for exploring the world around him. He appreciated the power of new experiences and meeting new people to develop his understanding of life, and himself, in the process. I feel I share his passion, aswell as an ironic sense of loss knowing that I can never possibly see and do it all. Nevertheless, I live my life with passion and excitement - spending my spare moments seeing concerts, hiking mountains, travelling, meeting new people, and occasionally undertaking new challenges. I'm reminded how diverse the human experience can be, and how small my window to the world must be. I'm often reminded to keep a curious and open mind.

My latest endeavor, into Full-Stack Web Development, has been a humbling experience. There is so much to learn, yet even with an unrelenting passion for development and growth, there will always be more to know. But, armed with determination and ingenuity, one can certainly achieve great things, and in the process be both an aid and an asset to those around them. That's the goal, both in life and in coding!
